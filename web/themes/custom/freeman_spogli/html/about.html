Partnership with Management

We are engaged, board-level investors, but we do not operate businesses.

We support our management teams from a strategic perspective in the growth of their businesses. We have developed significant resources in our target sectors, including a network of industry executives who serve as board-level resources to our partners.

Investment Criteria

Company Characteristics:

  • Leading businesses with transformative opportunities
  • Strong management teams
  • Defensible market positions
  • Platform for organic and acquisition growth
  • Solid margins and free cash flow

Geography:

  • North America

Transaction Size:

  • EBITDA: $10 - $75M
  • Equity Investments: $50 - $200M
  • TEV: $100 - $750M
Value Building

We work with management teams to develop and implement value-building strategies for our portfolio companies, including:

  • Strategic roadmaps
  • Operating enhancements
  • Management team additions
  • Add-on acquisitions

Through these programs, we provide our management teams with the support and capital to build larger, more competitive and more profitable businesses.
