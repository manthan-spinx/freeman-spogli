Bradford M. Freeman

Co-chairman

Bradford M. Freeman co-founded Freeman Spogli in 1983. Previously, he was a Managing Director of Dean Witter Reynolds Inc.'s Los Angeles Corporate Finance Department and a member of its Board of Directors.

In addition, Mr. Freeman was responsible for Dean Witter's international corporate finance activities, headquartered in London, from 1975 to 1976. Mr. Freeman earned a bachelor's degree with distinction from Stanford University in 1964 and a master's degree in business administration from Harvard University in 1966.
