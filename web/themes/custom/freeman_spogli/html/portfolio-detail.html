Planet Fitness

Formed in 2009 and headquartered in Fargo, ND, PF Baseline Fitness is a leading franchisee of Planet Fitness health clubs. Planet Fitness Inc., the franchisor, is one of the largest and fastest-growing franchisors and operators of fitness centers in the U.S. with more than 1,400 stores system-wide and over 10 million members. The concept caters to first-time or casual gym users by offering members a low-cost, high-quality experience.
